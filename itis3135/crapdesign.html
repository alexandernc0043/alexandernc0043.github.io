Good Design - Google

Popular search engine used by lots of people around the world.

  • C: Depending on your systems theme the site will either be in Dark mode or Light mode with the colors being dark grey on white or white on darkgrey
  • R: Very repetitive when searching with all the links looking the same.
  • A: Content is aligned left and is split up at the beginning sometimes with more information depending on the search.
  • P: Content has good spacing

Bad Design - TheBigUglyWebsite

Website designed to be as bad as possible.

  • C: No standard contrast and almost every element has different contrast/coloring.
  • R: Repetitive elements that go down the page that change slightly.
  • A: Aligned left
  • P: Everything is very close to each other
